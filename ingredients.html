Our Ingredients

We believe in the power of nature. Every ingredient is carefully selected for its purity, sustainability, and skin-nourishing benefits.

Olive oil

Sourced from sun-drenched Mediterranean groves, our organic olive oil is prized for its deeply nourishing properties. Rich in antioxidants and essential fatty acids, it helps to moisturize and protect the skin, leaving it soft, smooth, and revitalized. Its gentle, natural composition supports the skin barrier while promoting a healthy, radiant glow.

Found in: Soap

Castor oil

Sourced from carefully selected castor beans, our organic castor oil is celebrated for its deeply conditioning and restorative properties. Rich in ricinoleic acid and essential fatty acids, it helps to nourish and strengthen the skin while locking in long-lasting moisture. Its naturally soothing nature supports skin balance, leaving it soft, supple, and visibly healthy.

Found in: Soap

Botanical fruit peels

Sourced from carefully selected botanical fruit peels, this natural blend is rich in antioxidants and plant-based compounds known for their skin-renewing properties. Gently helping to refine and revitalize the skin, it supports natural exfoliation while promoting a clearer, smoother complexion. Its refreshing botanical profile enhances skin vitality, leaving it balanced, radiant, and visibly rejuvenated.

Found in: Soap

Essential oil

Sourced from carefully selected botanicals, our essential oils are valued for their concentrated aromatic and skin-enhancing properties. Extracted to preserve their natural integrity, they help to soothe the senses while supporting skin balance and overall well-being. Their delicate yet effective profile enhances relaxation, leaving both skin and mind feeling refreshed, restored, and harmonized.

Found in: Soap and diffuser
