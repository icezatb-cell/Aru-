Our Ingredients

We believe in the power of nature. Every ingredient is carefully selected for its purity, sustainability, and skin-nourishing benefits.

French Lavender

Sourced from the rolling hills of Provence, our organic lavender essential oil is renowned for its calming properties. It gently soothes irritated skin while its aromatic profile helps to reduce stress and promote restful sleep.

Found in: Wild Lavender Soap

Activated Charcoal

A powerful natural detoxifier. Activated charcoal acts like a magnet to draw out impurities, excess oil, and toxins from deep within the pores, leaving your skin deeply cleansed and refreshed without stripping its natural moisture.

Found in: Activated Charcoal Soap

French Pink Clay

The mildest of all clays, French Pink Clay is perfect for sensitive and mature skin. It gently exfoliates, improves circulation, and helps restore a healthy, radiant glow while maintaining the skin's delicate balance.

Found in: Rose & Pink Clay Soap

Eucalyptus Extract

Known for its invigorating and purifying qualities, eucalyptus helps to clear the mind and awaken the senses. Its natural antibacterial properties make it an excellent choice for deeply cleansing and refreshing the skin.

Found in: Eucalyptus Dew Soap
